ABOUT US

The MP Aajeevika Mart portal, launched under the guidance of the Honorable Chief Minister, empowers approximately three lakh women self-help groups and 33 lakh rural women members across the state. This platform facilitates increased participation and employment opportunities by enabling registered groups to showcase and sell their products to government and non-government organizations, as well as individual consumers.
