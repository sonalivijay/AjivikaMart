HAVE ANY QUESTIONS?

MP Aajeevika Mart is an initiative to promote and sell handmade and locally-produced items created by rural entrepreneurs and artisans in Madhya Pradesh, fostering sustainable livelihoods.

MP Aajeevika Mart is managed by government initiatives supporting rural entrepreneurship.

The platform offers a wide range of handmade products, including textiles, handicrafts, and organic food items.

MP Aajeevika Marts are located in various districts across Madhya Pradesh, providing accessibility to rural entrepreneurs.
